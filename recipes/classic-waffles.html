Classic Waffles

Description

These easy Classic Waffles make a tasty alternative to pancakes or French toast on those days when you just don't feel like making breakfast!
Crispy yet soft, sweet yet buttery - they're just amazing!

Ingredients

For the waffles

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1-3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt

For serving

  • butter
  • maple syrup
  • fruit

Steps

  1. Heat waffle iron.
  2. Beat eggs in a large bowl with a hand mixer until fluffy. Beat in the remaining ingredients just until smooth.
  3. Pour a scant 2/3 cup of the batter (see note below) onto the center of the hot waffle iron. Close the lid of the waffle iron.
  4. Bake for about 5 minutes or until the steaming stops. Carefully remove the waffle.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 with the remaining batter.
  6. Serve the waffles with butter, maple syrup, fruit, whipped cream, or your favorite t.oppings
