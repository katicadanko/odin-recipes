Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Description

Healthy broccoli cheddar soup packed with carrots, broccoli, garlic, and cheese.
This creamy velvety soup is much better than Paneraâ€™s broccoli cheddar soup and can be made in under 30 minutes for a fraction of the price!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 medium onion chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic minced
  • 4 tablespoon AP flour
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 large carrot
  • 2 cups half & half
  • 2 cups block grated cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large dutch oven or pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook 3-4 minutes or until softened and light gold. Add the garlic and saute for another minute.
  2. Add flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes or until the flour begins to turn golden in color. Pour in the chicken stock, broccoli florets, carrots, and seasoning.
    Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15 minutes or until the broccoli and carrots are cooked through.
  3. Stir in half & half and cheddar cheese and simmer for another minute. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  4. Serve with toasted crusty bread or in a bread bowl if desired.
