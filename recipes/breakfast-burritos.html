Breakfast Burritos

Description

Breakfast Burritos are the perfect breakfast! Loaded with potatoes, eggs, and either ham, bacon or sausage, these breakfast burritos are the best way to start your day! Double the batch and freeze to save time on busy mornings!

Ingredients

  • breakfast sausage
  • bacon
  • ham
  • potatoes
  • cheese
  • tortillas
  • eggs
  • milk
  • olive oil
  • seasoning salt
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the protein by cooking the bacon, browning the sausage, and frying the ham.
  2. Put the potatoes in the oven to bake.
  3. Make scrambled eggs, and warm up the tortillas.
  4. Build the burritos by setting up an assembly line with all the ingredients.
